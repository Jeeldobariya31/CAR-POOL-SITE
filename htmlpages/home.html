Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to carpool with strangers?

All users are verified and rated. You can check profiles and reviews before sharing a ride.

How do I pay for my ride?

Payment can be arranged directly with the driver or through our secure platform (coming soon!).

Can I offer a ride if I don't have a fixed schedule?

Yes! You can post rides as per your convenience and connect with riders who match your timing.

Safety Tips

Always verify the profile and rating of your co-rider or driver.
Share your trip details with a friend or family member.
Meet in public places for pick-up and drop-off.
Follow all traffic rules and respect each other.
